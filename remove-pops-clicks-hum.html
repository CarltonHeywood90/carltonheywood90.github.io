Why Clean Audio Matters

Pops, clicks, and hum can ruin an otherwise perfect recording. Whether it’s an old vinyl transfer, podcast, or music track, these imperfections distract listeners and reduce the impact of your content. I specialize in restoring audio so it sounds smooth, clear, and professional.

What I Can Fix

  • Pops & Clicks: Eliminating transient noises from vinyl, cassette, or digital recordings.
  • Hum & Buzz: Removing electrical interference and background hum without affecting the original sound.
  • General Restoration: Cleaning up hiss, crackle, and other unwanted artifacts for a polished final product.

How the Process Works

  1. Submit Your Recording: Send your audio in any standard format (WAV, MP3, AIFF).
  2. Evaluation: I analyze your track to identify problem areas and the best restoration approach.
  3. Restoration: Using professional software, I carefully remove pops, clicks, hum, and other artifacts.
  4. Delivery: Receive your clean, polished audio ready for distribution or personal use.
