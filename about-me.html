About Us

Brutal Honesty, Relentless Drive (BHRD) is hope without expectation. It’s about being honest with yourself about where you are and your prospects — but still charging toward your goals with relentless determination.

BHRD’s music is instrumental progressive metal, blending theory, technique, groove, brutality, and the unique personalities of its members to create chaotic, immersive soundscapes. Every riff, rhythm, and crescendo is designed to push boundaries — both musically and mentally.

The core message is simple: Go for it. Whether you’re facing obstacles in life or in art, BHRD’s music is a soundtrack for perseverance. Borrow the intensity of the sound to build momentum and keep moving forward.

Current members include Max Dail on drums and Carlton Heywood on guitar. BHRD is actively seeking a bassist to complete the lineup and expand its sonic impact.
